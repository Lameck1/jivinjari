jivinjari: a one stop directory for tourism destinations in kenya.

to "jivinjari", follow these simple steps

1- search:

The jivinjari homepage having loaded, use the seach-bar above to enter a type of tourism destinations that your heart is after. Click on the search icon next to it. This will give you a a list of search results as per search category. To view all available options, just click on the search icon without entering any text.

2- locate:

From the selected search result in step one, you will be redirected to a detail page which has an embeded google map from which you can get location specifics and decide whether that will be a good fit for your travel needs.

3- book:

While on the detail page, you will see a "BOOK "button, use it if you would wish to do booking online with us. You can also do booking by dialing the number present on the detail page. The type of booking you will do is with travel agency that will take care of your travel and boarding needs.

4- jivinjari(enjoy):

Now that all your travel logistics is sorted out, sit back, relax and enjoy when your d-day finally comes.
